How it works

Sense. Predict. Act.

01 / SENSE

It reads the room

Every 20 minutes, each Vex captures temperature, humidity and gas, the signals that precede mould.

02 / PREDICT

Our model scores risk

A proprietary algorithm turns readings into a live, room-level mould-risk score that sharpens over time.

03 / ACT

Everyone's alerted

Tenants get guidance in-app. Landlords see rising-risk units on one dashboard, with timestamped evidence that keeps them compliant with the law and clear of heavy fines.
